# Silverguard Skybow

### Purpose
Each member of the Silverguard has a special type of skybow. While it is similar to a [normal skybow](skybow.md) in many ways, it does have several more advanced features.
### Physical Description
For the most part, including materials, these bows are the same as the [normal skybow](skybow.md). The differences come into play when we see that instead of just a spear point, both sides have a sharpened blade. This effectively allows the bow to be wielded as a double bladed sword with a handle in the middle.
### Abilities
#### Split
The Silverguard skybow can have its string retracted into one side of the shaft after being disconnected from the other, and then have the two halves separated into what is essentially two shortswords.
