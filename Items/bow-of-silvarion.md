# Bow of Silvarion

### History
An ancient member of the elven Silverguard named Silvarion had his skybow modified and personalized extensively. One of the things he did was pay a countless amount of money to have elf enchanters give the bow near invincibility, immune to deterioration and resistent to stress. Silvarion later came to be one of the best warriors the elves had ever seen, but sadly died valiently defending Halvarn during the final battle in the War of Fallen Glory. Elder Evron of the elves kept Silvarion's skybow for almost two centuries until he met a young boy named Darian and gifted him the bow.
### Physical Description
For the most part, the Bow of Silvarion simply looks like a regular [Silverguard's skybow](generic/silverguard-skybow.md). However, upon deeper inspection, many of the modifications show. For starters, the bow comes fitted with shield that is ready to spring out of hiding in one of the shaft halves and protect the bowman at a moment's notice. It also has several small holes that, along with the special enchantments, can gust wind out at anybody who gets too close.
### Abilities
In addition to the regular abilities of a Silverguard skybow, the Bow of Silvarion has severl unique abilities, both magical and not.
#### Gale Draw
If the bow is fully drawn and held, wind energy will start to gather around the arrow. The longer it is held, the more energy. This energy causes the arrow to pick up extra velocity and range from the wind.
#### Tempest Shot
Wind energy can be channeled into the bow frame, causing the arrow to be surrounded by a swirling vortext that can knock enemies near the path back significantly.
#### Gust Wall
Wind can be blasted out of small holes all throughout the bow shaft at high speeds, allowing for an effective deterrent for lightweight projectiles, and even slowing some enemies.
#### Pop Shield
A small, personal shield can be instantly popped out of the bow's shaft, allowing for quick defense when a conventional shield is not quickly accessible.
