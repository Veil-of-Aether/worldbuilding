# Sword of the Elements

### History
After the four guardians were selected, the Spirits guided them to forge a blade. Together, the guardians put together this mythical obsidian sword. The Spirits then blessed it, giving it sentience, the ability to transform into many shapes, and the property of being unbreakable. It was then laid to rest in a pedestal in the Shrine of Elements, ready to be drawn by any who needed its power.

### Physical Description
The Sword of the Elements is a broadsword with a thin obsidian blade, however, the blade has the ability to transform into other shapes including a heavy claymore, a curved scimitar, and a long saber. The hilt is made of pure gold, and the handle is wrapped in Wind Silk. While in the normal form or any other form with a crossguard, each side of the crossguard has two holes for gems to be placed in, and there are enchanted gems in the colors of brown, white, red, and blue in the holes.

### Abilities
#### Sentience
The sword has a mind of its own, as it has limited sentience. The blade can communicate telepathically to its master as well as somewhat influence its movements and shapeshift into a different type of sword at will. When it is drawn, it can also look into the mind of the would-be wielder and see whether they have any malicious intentions. If so, it can reject them as master and activate the curse, in which case the hilt becomes unbearably hot and the gems giving it many of its other abilities break away and travel to their respective temples, as each gem corresponds to an element.
#### Shapeshifting
The sword can change its shape into many different types of swords including a claymore, a scimitar, a saber, a longsword, and a very short and dull knife. It uses the knife form when its master is trying to do something the sword deems evil, in which case it does this to resist.
#### Earth
The ability given by the Earth Gem can cause shockwaves in the ground around the wielder that stun some enemies. It can also send a more concentrated wave through the ground in one direction that can knock over some enemies and stuns a wider range.
#### Fire
The Fire Gem gives the ability to charge the blade with fire before a swing. It also lets the sword throw what are essentially sword beams made of fire.
#### Water
If the Water Gem is present, it passively allows for better attacks while underwater, but also gives the ability to extinguish fire and to freeze enemies and objects in ice.
#### Wind
The Wind Gem allows for charged attacks, which can use the power of wind to lunge with greater power and strength, or to allow spin attacks. These attacks cause higher damage, more knockback, and often a smoother cutting motion.
#### Sacred Purification
When all four gems are in the hilt, the sword glows faintly all the time. More importantly, the sword will purify corrupted Light that it comes across. Corrupted Light, which is used to power dark magic, will be converted into pure Light.
