# Garret
### Darian's granfather, commander, deceased

Garret was a commander of the earthguard before his death. When he went to investigate a disturbance in the Forest Sanctuary with some of the earthguard, including his son. Unfortunately, he was killed defending his son, who was also gravely injured and died not long after. Their deaths led Garret's son's wife to die of grief, leaving the young Darian with his grandmother and Garret's wife, Elda.
