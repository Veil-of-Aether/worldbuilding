# Dolmordai
## "King Beneath all Shadows
### Demon King

Dolmordai is the primordial evil in Neyvara. As elder of the Celestial Beings, he became tired of doing nothing and started corrupting Light, the very essence of life. He soon created a stone called Ersund out of pure darkness, and with it, he corrupted his entire race, becoming the demons. Dolmordai, then planted this stone on Neyvara's surface, and it began to spread its corruption across the mortal races, causing them to fight among themselves. After millenia of waiting, he mustered his malevolent forces and began his attack on the mortals. He did not expect them to put aside their differences, ally, and defeat him. For years, his soul lived on, waiting for a willing host. That is when he found Veyric. Near the end of the game, Dolmordai manages to almost completely corrupt the Sacred Light, and in doing so unravel the Veil of Aether, which would collapse reality.
