# Korric
### Forgemaster Apprentice

Korric is a new apprentice to Forgemaster Borin. He just does it to have something to do though, and shows no passion for his job. His mother is constantly sick, so he often goes near the border of Wind Province to collect rare plants for her. He started eyeing a young elf named Selira who often sits by the border, but lacks the confidence to talk to her.
