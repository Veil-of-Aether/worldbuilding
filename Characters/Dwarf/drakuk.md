# Drak'uk takh Morvath-Krakh
### First Elder

Drak'uk was the first elder of the dwarves. After being appointed by the Spirits themselves, he got to work. He commissioned the building of a manor in his new capitol city of Morvath-Krakh, or better known by others as Embercradle. He also wielded a massive hammer made out of the strongest forged dwarven steel, and it became a legendary weapon that seemed to have mysteriously vanished after his death.
