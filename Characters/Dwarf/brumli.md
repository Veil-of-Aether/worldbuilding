# Brumli

Brumli is a somewhat impulsive dwarf who kind of likes a good fight. A member of the Shrouded Flame, Kaledir, caught Brumli as he was trying to raid a demon camp and convinced the brash dwarf to talk to Torric. Not long after, Brumli was officially a member of the Shrouded Flame. While in the group, he often clashed with both Selene and Vaelith, but eventually warmed up to them both and ended up falling in love with Selene.
