# Kaledir
### Merfolk elite

Kaledir is the son of Elder Tibor. This merfolk joined the army and began training as an elite as soon as he was old enough to do so. As Veyric rose, Kaledir was one of the strongest voices speaking out for fighting against Veyric, but to no avail. Torric tracked him down and convinced Kaledir to join his resistance group, the Shrouded Flame.
