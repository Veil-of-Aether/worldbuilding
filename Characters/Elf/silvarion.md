# Silvarion
### Silverguard Elite

Widely known as the best warrior mortals had ever seen, Silvarion wielded a heavily customized Silverguard skybow as his primary weapon. He was unfortunately killed along with most of the rest of the Silverguard while defending Halvarn at the Shrine of the Elements during the final battle in the War of Fallen Glory. He did not die in vain, as without Halvarn staying protected, the guardians and dragons never would have joined the battle in destroying Dolmordai.
