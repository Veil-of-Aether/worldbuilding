# Vaelith
### Elf War Veteran

Vaelith is a veteran of the War of Fallen Glory. His brother and father were both killed in this great conflict, which left Vaelith with a bitter resentment for anything to do with demons or dark magic. Elder Torric of the men found out about Vaelith and reached out to him about joining the Shrouded Flame. Vaelith jumped as this opportunity, as he held a deep hatred for Veyric.
