# Zephyr
## “Wind Enchantress”
### Wind Guardian

Zephyr serves as the guardian of Wind Province. She is generally quiet and rarely speaks, but when she speaks, she is usually seen as very wise. She is well known in all of Neyvara for this great wisdom as well as her beauty. As a guardian, she resides in the Wind Temple sleeping until awakened. She was possessed by Veyric's creation - Typhon - upon the sorcerer's rise to power, but is released by a young man named Darian.
