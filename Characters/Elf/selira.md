# Selira
### Great granddaughter of Evron

Selira is a young elf girl who has always held a fascination with dwarven culture, despite her race's historical resentment of dwarves. She often sits by a waterfall near the border between Wind and Fire Provinces, watching. It was here that she first laid eyes on a young dwarf named Korric, who frequented that area to collect rare plants native to Wind Province for his sick mother. Selira soon grew fond of this young dwarf and hopes to talk to him, but fears what may happen if she is caught talking to a dwarf.
