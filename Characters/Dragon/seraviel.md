# Seraviel
## “Healing Light”
### Elymion's advisor

Seraviel was an advisor to Elymion. He watched over the affairs of the world with silent dignity, but he lacked great wisdom. This lack of wisdom often led to pride, and that would be his downfall.

# Seravokh
## “Corrupted Light”
### Watcher of Ersund

Seraviel proudly sought to subdue Ersund and use its power against Dolmordai, but in doing so, he became corrupted by its seducing power. He became Seravokh, cursed to guard Ersund from any who would try to touch it. He would, however, be forgiven by Elymion after Darian defeats him in battle and destroys Ersund, breaking the chains on Seravokh.
