# Blackheart Keep

Blackheart Keep was a dungeon/torture chamber owned by Dolmordai. The building is situated in southern Wind Province and heavily fortified. The gates have been placed under a curse that prevents them from being opened by anyone other than demons. Inside, the Warden of Chains kept watch over the prisoners, and an army of other phantom beasts ensured that none escaped.

During the present time, Darian may optionally take on what's left of the Keep as a dungeon and fight the resurrected Warden of Chains.
