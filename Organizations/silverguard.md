# Silverguard

### Description
The Silverguard is a group of six of the most elite elves and are trusted as the elder's bodyguards and the ones who do his most personal missions. These elves are rarely switched out except in the event that one passes away, and these warriors are incredibly scary for any who aren't on good terms with the elven elder. Each member wears flexible wind silk robes over silver chainmail. They each carry several small daggers, a specialized Silverguard skybow, a longsword, and plenty of arrows. A Silverguard is chosen when there is a spot open, and then the best of the best from the elven elite corps is selected. These few individuals go through extreme loyalty tests as well as extra training before finally being initiated into the group.
