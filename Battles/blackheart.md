# Storming of Blackheart

The war had been raging for over a year now, and Halvarn decided that Blackheart Keep had been in operation for too long. He wanted to free those being tortured in that cursed dungeon. The attack started with elven windriders soaring high above and dwarves tunneling below. Eventually, the dwarves broke through the solid floor of the central chamber. From here, elitists and dwarves started swarming the hall. Elite elves and merfolk took on the Phantom beasts while the dwarves climbed high and collapsed the roof. From here, elven windriders were able to come in and collapse a wall. From this point, the entire army of the Alliance swarmed the prison. The battle was gruesome and brutal, but in the end there were relatively few casualties on the side of the Alliance, and many prisoners who survived the harsh environment were freed.
